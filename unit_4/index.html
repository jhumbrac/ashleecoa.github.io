ABOUT

Ash the Floet is a blogger, poet, and screenwriter. She was born and raised in Texas and obtained her degree in Journalism and Broadcasting. Ash the Floet creates work that is raw and powerful, focusing on topics like dating and relationships, feminism, racial and religious discrimination, body image, and love. She is currently working and writing in Texas with a hope to one day change the world with her words.
